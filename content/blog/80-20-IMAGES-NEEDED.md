## Tools to Create Images

**Option 1: Canva** (Recommended for non-designers)
- Use "Custom Size" 1200x600 for headers
- Search templates for "infographic" or "process diagram"
- Export as PNG

**Option 2: Figma** (If you're design-savvy)
- More control over design
- Can match your exact brand
- Export as PNG or WebP

**Option 3: PowerPoint/Keynote**
- Quick and familiar
- Export slides as images
- Easy to make simple diagrams

**Option 4: Hire on Fiverr**
- Quick turnaround (24-48 hours)
- Professional results
- Cost: $20-50 per image
- Search for "infographic design" or "blog post graphics"
